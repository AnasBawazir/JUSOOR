About Us

Jusoor Hub is a fast-growing hassle-free fulfillment service provider under the HSS Logistics portfolio with a purpose of providing a first-in-class remarkable experience for our clients. Since the beginning, we have fully understood the critical role we play as an extension of our clients’ business. For that reason, we continuously strive for unparalleled excellence and we aim to go above and beyond expectations to ensure that we will collectively thrive and succeed.

MISSION & VISION

We recognize that the success of our business depends on the success of our partners. We strive to enable that success by delivering our partner's products to their customers quickly, accurately, and with premium quality.

MISSION

To be the partner-of-choice for best-in-class e-commerce fulfillment services.

VISION

Enabling tomorrow's players today.
